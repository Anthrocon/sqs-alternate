Accessibility at Anthrocon

Our convention strives to provide options and services to help improve your experience.

Wheelchairs & Mobility - Accessibility at the Venue - Service Animals - Sign Language Interpreters

Wheelchairs & Mobility Scooters

A limited number of wheelchairs are available for loan by the Convention Center on a first-come, first-served basis. They are available at the East Lobby security desk, West Lobby security desk, and the Spirit of Pittsburgh Ballroom, Presented by Dollar Bank, security desk. Wheelchairs are not permitted to leave the property.

Attendees who require mobility assistance and are unable to bring such may wish to consider renting the necessary equipment from such establishments as Steel Town Mobility or Washington Medical Equipment.

Accessibility at the venue

The David L. Lawrence Convention Center, the Westin Convention Center, and all of Anthrocon's partner hotels are fully accessible and compliant with the Americans with Disabilities Act.

  • For Accessibility information at the David L. Lawrence Center, please see the DLCC Accessibility page.

  • For Accessibility information at the hotel or accommodation you are staying at, please visit the hotel’s website.

Service Animals

Service animals are welcome in the David L. Lawrence Convention Center provided that they are in compliance with regulations established by the U.S. Dept. of Justice, as defined in the ADA (Service Animals). All service animals must remain on a leash or in a harness, and be under control of the guest with a disability or a designee.

Please note: animals whose sole function is to provide comfort or emotional support do not qualify as service animals under the ADA.

Sign Language Interpreters

Most main stage events and select smaller events are accompanied by professional sign language interpreters using American Sign Language (ASL). If you have questions regarding interpreting services or would like to request an accommodation, please let us know by emailing [email protected].

Additional Accessibility Requests

If you have a request that you would like us to consider, please let us know by emailing [email protected]. While in some cases we may be able to provide additional accommodation and will give every request very careful consideration, we cannot guarantee that individual requests can be accommodated.
