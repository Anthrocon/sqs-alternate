Booking an Anthrocon Hotel Room

We have 12 Anthrocon-partnered hotels offering special rates for our attendees, all within the locale of the David L. Lawrence Convention Center. A valid Anthrocon registration ID will be required to make a hotel reservation - without it, your reservation may not be honored. Register for the con on our Registration page.

Booking process - Hotel options - FAQs

Booking process for 2023

Booking for Anthrocon’s hotel rooms will begin in January 2023. Please read through the entire process to understand what’s changing for 2023. The most important thing to note is that you need to have registered for Anthrocon in order to access the hotel booking system and reserve a room.

  • Starting in January 2023, the link for general hotel reservations will be sent to the email that you have registered with - do NOT book through a hotel’s website. Reservations must be made through our booking link in order to get the convention rate - if you make a reservation outside of Anthrocon's hotel block, it cannot be transferred into Anthrocon’s block will be ineligible for the discounted rate.

  • For those who register before January 30th, an email will be sent out to your registered email when general hotel reservations open containing the link needed to reserve a hotel room. If you register after that date, the link needed to reserve a hotel room will be included in the confirmation email from your registration.

  • When reserving a hotel room, you will be required to enter your Registrant ID from your Anthrocon registration. This number can be found on your registration confirmation email as “Registrant ID”.

  • Only one room may be booked per registered attendee, as it is linked to the registration.

  • A limited number of rooms will be set aside for people who are eligible for advance hotel booking (such as Supersponsors). This is to allow attendees a chance to reserve a room at each hotel without having a hotel fill up before general opening. We will send the general hotel reservation link to all registered attendees on February 2, so you will have a second chance if you do not get the hotel room of your choice during advance booking.

  • A one-night deposit will NOT be required this year. However, a credit card must be linked to the room reservation at the time of booking. Cancellations or changes can be made without penalty until March 31 - after that date, cancellations will be charged a one-night penalty (room and tax) at the hotel’s rate.

The hotel reservation block will be opening as follows:

  • Ultrasponsors: January 25th, 2023 at approx. 11:00 AM EST

  • Dealers: January 27th, 2023 at approx. 11:00 AM EST

  • Supersponsors: January 30th, 2023 at approx. 11:00 AM EST

  • General: February 2nd, 2023 at approx. 11:00 AM EST

The times shown indicate the times that emails will be sent to people in each sub-block. Anthrocon is not responsible for lost or undeliverable emails. Check your spam folders if you do not receive the email at the listed time.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • There will not be a deposit taken this year; instead, we are giving people until March 31st, 2023 to cancel a reservation without penalty. After that date, any cancellations will be assessed a one-night cancellation fee at the hotel’s rate. A credit card number is still required to make a reservation.

    A valid Anthrocon registration ID is required to make a hotel reservation - you will NOT be able to access the hotel booking system without it.

  • Anthrocon strongly recommends that its members not book a room until they are reasonably certain that their plans will not change.

    Reservations may be changed or canceled by contacting [email protected], or by phone at +1-800-470-8077, without any penalty until March 31st, 2023. After that date, a one-night penalty will be charged to the cardholder on the reservation.

  • Yes, the minimum age to reserve a hotel room is 18.

  • Rooms are booked very quickly due to high demand, and we try to make as many rooms available on the opening date. There are separate advance blocks depending on your registration type - if you are unable to secure a room in an earlier block, you can try again when the general block opens.

    If you’re unable to get the reservation you want, we would recommend securing a room at other hotels to ensure you have a place to stay during the convention. Alternatively, you can also try and find rooming arrangements with other attendees that have space (you can find these on various forums and websites).

    A room may show as "not available" when only one of the nights is truly not available. This is likely to happen for the first or last night of your stay, or If you try to book a date outside the convention time frame. Try requesting a shorter stay, and if you are then able to make a successful reservation, contact [email protected] (or call +1-800-470-8077) and indicate that you're looking to add additional nights.

    Please note: The higher demand hotels (such as the Westin, Courtyard, and the Drury) will have a number of rooms set aside for the general reservation opening.  This is done to ensure that no hotel sells out completely before the general opening and to give all attendees a chance to reserve a room in the higher demand hotels. As such you may see a hotel sell out during the advanced reservation period but once again have rooms available during general reservations.

    In the event that you wish to transfer your room reservation and linked registration ID to another person staying in your room, please contact [email protected] to inform them of the transfer request.

  • No, you are only allowed to book one room per registration. We will be conducting checks to ensure that attendees are not booking multiple rooms under the same registration to keep the booking system fair for all attendees.

  • Drop by our Anthrocon Room Share group on Facebook to get started! Or, if Facebook is not your thing, we also have our Discord channel #roomshare on the Official Anthrocon Discord Server.

  • If you encounter any issues while trying to reserve a hotel room, please contact [email protected]. They can also be contacted by phone at +1-800-470-8077. Anthrocon cannot address issues with the reservation system as it is operated and maintained by Visit Pittsburgh.
